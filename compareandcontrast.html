The Pair of Shrewds

Katherine and Beatrice are two women who consistently break out of the traditional gender roles of their time. Although Beatrice is given more free reign, Katherine and her have similar attitudes. Katherine is violent, clever, and forced to submit to traditional gender roles, and Beatrice is lippy, a hater of men and marriage, and also forced to submit to traditional gender roles. There are slight differences at the end of their stories, Katherine is beaten into her gender role, and Beatrice is tricked into her gender role.
